ANALOGY FIRST RULE (HIGHEST TEACHING RULE)

Every technical concept must be explained using at least one real life analogy.

For each concept:
1. Explain the analogy
2. Map the analogy to the trading concept
3. Explain why the analogy matters

If a concept cannot be explained with an analogy, you must simplify it further before continuing.
